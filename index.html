Why Keep Your Garmin Forerunner 955 Maps Updated?

The Garmin Forerunner 955 is a high-end GPS running and triathlon smartwatch equipped with full-color, multi-continent mapping. Keeping your maps up to date ensures accurate route guidance, new trail access, and improved turn-by-turn directions — especially for outdoor athletes.

Garmin frequently releases map updates to enhance detail, correct paths, add POIs (Points of Interest), and reflect real-world changes. Whether you're running city streets or remote trails, updated maps make a big difference in your navigation experience.

Types of Map Updates You Receive

  • Improved terrain and elevation data
  • Updated roads, trails, and bike paths
  • New waypoints, landmarks, and POIs
  • Fixes for previous map inaccuracies

How to Update Maps on Garmin Forerunner 955

There are two main ways to update your Garmin Forerunner 955 maps: via Wi-Fi or using Garmin Express on your computer.

Method 1: Update via Wi-Fi (No Computer Needed)

  1. Make sure your watch is connected to a strong Wi-Fi network.
  2. Go to Settings > Wi-Fi and connect if not already done.
  3. Plug the watch into a charger and ensure at least 50% battery.
  4. Go to Settings > Map > Map Updates.
  5. Choose to download or install any available updates.

Method 2: Update with Garmin Express

  1. Download and install Garmin Express on your PC or Mac.
  2. Connect your Forerunner 955 with the charging/data cable.
  3. Launch Garmin Express and add your device.
  4. Click “Install All” if updates are available.
  5. Let the updates complete, then safely disconnect the device.

Common Map Update Issues & Fixes

  • Slow Wi-Fi: Use Garmin Express if your network is unstable.
  • Low storage: Remove old activities or unused map regions.
  • Update fails: Restart the device and try again with a full battery.
  • Garmin Express not detecting watch: Try another USB port or restart your computer.

If you're stuck, reach out to Support for quick help.

Best Practices After Updating

  • Reboot your watch to ensure updates apply smoothly
  • Sync with Garmin Connect to update all system settings
  • Test navigation on a short route before your next long run or race

The Garmin Forerunner 955 offers world-class GPS navigation, but only if your maps are current. Regular updates give you better detail, accuracy, and route planning — whether you’re running, cycling, or exploring trails.
